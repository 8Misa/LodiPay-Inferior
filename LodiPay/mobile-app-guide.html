Quick Transfers

Send money to friends and family in seconds with just their phone number or email.

  1. Tap "Send" on the home screen
  2. Enter recipient's contact info
  3. Input amount and add note
  4. Confirm with fingerprint or PIN

QR Code Payments

Pay at merchants or receive money using our secure QR code system.

  1. Select "QR Code" from menu
  2. Scan merchant's QR code to pay
  3. Show your QR code to receive money
  4. Confirm transactions instantly

Biometric Security

Secure your app with fingerprint, face recognition, or PIN protection.

  1. Enable biometric in Settings
  2. Set up your preferred method
  3. Use for quick authentication
  4. Manage security settings anytime

Real-time Notifications

Stay informed with instant alerts for all your transactions and account activity.

  1. Customize notification preferences
  2. Enable push notifications
  3. Receive instant transaction alerts
  4. Get security notifications

Financial Insights

Track your spending habits and manage your budget with detailed analytics.

  1. Access Analytics from menu
  2. View spending categories
  3. Set budget goals
  4. Receive monthly reports

Card Management

Link and manage all your payment cards directly from the app.

  1. Go to Cards section
  2. Add new card securely
  3. Set default payment method
  4. Freeze/unfreeze cards instantly

Pro Tips for Using the App

Maximize your LodiPay experience with these helpful tips:

  • Enable Dark Mode - Reduce eye strain in low-light conditions by switching to dark theme in Settings
  • Use Widgets - Add LodiPay widgets to your home screen for quick access to balances and transfers
  • Set Up Quick Actions - Configure shortcuts for your most frequent transactions
  • Enable Location Services - Find nearby ATMs and partner merchants instantly
  • Regular Updates - Keep your app updated to enjoy the latest features and security enhancements
