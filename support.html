Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. You can also change your password in the Settings section under Security.

What are the transaction fees?

LodiPay charges a small fee for certain transactions. Domestic transfers are free for Premium users, while standard users pay ₱15 per transaction. International transfers have a 1% fee with a minimum of ₱100. Bills payments are free of charge.

How long does it take to process transfers?

Domestic transfers are processed instantly. International transfers typically take 1-3 business days. Bills payments are usually processed within 24 hours. Processing times may vary depending on the recipient's bank or service provider.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data. All transactions are monitored for fraud, and we comply with international security standards. Your personal and financial information is never shared with third parties without your consent.
