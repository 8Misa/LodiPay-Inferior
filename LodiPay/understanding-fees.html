Transparent Fee Structure

Understand all fees associated with using LodiPay services. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

Domestic Transfers

₱0.00

Send money to other LodiPay users within the Philippines with no transaction fees.

Examples:

  • Sending ₱1,000 to a friend: ₱0.00
  • Sending ₱5,000 to family: ₱0.00
  • Requesting payments: ₱0.00

International Transfers

1.0%

Low-cost international money transfers with a minimum fee of ₱100.

Examples:

  • Sending $100 USD: ₱55.00 (~₱100)
  • Sending €500 EUR: ₱300.00 (~₱100)
  • Sending ¥10,000 JPY: ₱450.00 (~₱100)

Card Payments

₱0.00

No fees for using your linked debit or credit cards for transactions.

Examples:

  • Adding funds with card: ₱0.00
  • Online purchases: ₱0.00
  • Bill payments: ₱0.00

Bills Payment

₱0.00

Pay your utilities, subscriptions, and other bills without any service charges.

Examples:

  • Electricity bill: ₱0.00
  • Water bill: ₱0.00
  • Internet subscription: ₱0.00

Cash In/Out

₱15.00

Small fee for cash transactions at partner locations (banks, convenience stores).

Examples:

  • Cash-in at 7-Eleven: ₱15.00
  • Bank deposit: ₱15.00
  • ATM withdrawal: ₱15.00

Savings & Investments

0.5%

Minimal management fees for savings and investment products to help your money grow.

Examples:

  • Monthly savings fee: 0.5% of balance
  • Investment management: 0.5% annually
  • Premium accounts: No additional fees
