Important Security Notice

LodiPay will never ask for your password, PIN, or security codes via email or phone. If you receive such requests, do not respond and report them immediately to our support team.

Use Strong Passwords

Create unique passwords with at least 12 characters including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

  • Never reuse passwords across accounts
  • Change passwords every 90 days
  • Use a password manager for secure storage

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security by requiring a second form of verification for login attempts.

  • Use authenticator apps instead of SMS
  • Backup your recovery codes
  • Enable 2FA on all important accounts

Secure Your Devices

Protect all devices used to access your LodiPay account with up-to-date security software.

  • Keep OS and apps updated
  • Use device encryption
  • Lock devices with strong passcodes

Be Cautious With Networks

Avoid accessing sensitive financial information on public Wi-Fi networks or unsecured connections.

  • Use VPN on public networks
  • Prefer cellular data for transactions
  • Verify network authenticity

Identify Phishing Attempts

Learn to recognize fraudulent emails, texts, and calls attempting to steal your information.

  • Check sender email addresses carefully
  • Never click suspicious links
  • Verify requests through official channels

Monitor Account Activity

Regularly review your transaction history and account settings for any unauthorized changes.

  • Enable transaction alerts
  • Review statements monthly
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
